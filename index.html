FAQ

Q: What is Universal Interval Timer, PWA Edition?

Universal Interval Timer is an app enabling you to create your own Interval Training exercises.
You can use it to build your custom HIIT session! Moreover, the application is preset with the famous "7 Min Workout" :)

You will be able to define:
- The exercise name
- The number of rounds
- The Warm up time
- The Work time
- The Rest time
- The movements you want to perform during the exercise. When the app will be running, it will display the movement to execute at the specified round

Q: How to create a new exercise?

From the main page click/tap on the "Add" link

Q: How to edit an exercise?

From the main page click/tap on the "Edit" link

Q: How many excercises can be saved?

With the basic version you can save up to 3 exercises

Q: How many movements by exercise can be saved?

With the basic version you can save up to 40 movements by exercise

Q: Where are the exercises stored?

The exercises that you define are stored locally in your browser. You can reset the exercise list to the default by cliking on the setting button at the top of the page and selecting "Reset exercise list"
